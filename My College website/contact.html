Government City College Details

Government City College was established in 1985 and is affiliated with a recognized state university. The college is accredited for maintaining quality education standards. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in Science, Commerce, and Arts. Facilities include a library, computer laboratories, sports grounds, smart classrooms, and Wi-Fi campus.
